My Mission

Turning creativity and technology into human centered digital experiences. My mission is to transform bold ideas into immersive digital experiences that connect creativity, technology, and purpose. I believe technology should feel intuitive, expressive, and supportive, not cold, invisible, or disconnected from the people it serves. As the founder of DCENTRIC, I work at the intersection of creative technology and visual storytelling, designing and developing modern web experiences, interactive platforms, and media driven solutions that are both technically sound and emotionally engaging. My work spans web development, motion and interaction design, videography, photography, and emerging AI powered systems, allowing me to approach every project from both a creative and engineering perspective. Through projects such as V.I.B.E. (Very Intelligent Buddy for Everything), do re mi, and The Deception of Delilah, I explore how technology can foster creativity, collaboration, and emotional clarity while respecting privacy, ownership, and user autonomy. These projects reflect my belief that innovation should enhance human experience rather than replace it, and that powerful tools can still feel personal, ethical, and thoughtfully designed. I am driven by continuous learning, experimentation, and the pursuit of meaningful impact. By pushing creative and technical boundaries responsibly, I aim to build work that inspires curiosity, empowers others to bring their ideas to life, and contributes to a more intentional and human centered digital future.
